googlechart v0.1.0 -- demo gallery

Fourteen chart types from the Google Visualization API, wrapped for Stata. Texas 2036 brand (Montserrat + navy/orange) applied throughout. Requires network at view time -- googlechart loads google.charts/loader.js from gstatic.com per Google ToS.

Jump to: 1. Diverging stacked bar (Pew-style Likert) 2. Likert long-form (searchable table) 3. Bar (horizontal, with data labels) 4. Line (multi-series) 5. Pie (legend below, square frame) 6.
